SOMETHING BIG


As many cancer patients misses their chemotherapy sessions due to lack of social supporting, we made this website to donate your free time to help out and support those patients, so join our community of life changers.

Cancer diagnosis is an important event in an individual’s life which has considerable outcomes and implications for patient and his/her family. Meanwhile, receiving assistance and support from family and friends has a significant impact on patients to help them to cope with disease-related stress and its treatment.
